Simon Goldsmith is a Chief Information Security Officer and a lifelong student of leadership and building high performing teams. Born into a family of big infrastructure engineering, his career has been defined less by a master plan and more by a mission to deliver positive impact at scale. Simon believes in security as both a profession and craft - one that requires relentless curiosity, the humility to listen to those in the code, and the agility to adapt to the unique complexity of the organisations and people we serve. 

Simon co-authored the ISO DevOps standard, is a founding consortium member of AIUC-1, and both a Chartered Cyber Security Professional and a Chartered Engineer. He is a passionate advocate for security as a team sport and makes it a weekly priority to improve diversity and inclusion in the industry.
